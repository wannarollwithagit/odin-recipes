Guacamole

Description

My own homemade guacamole!

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Red onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Mash avocado in a bowl with a fork.
  2. Mince onion, and add to avocado with the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Mix, and add extra of each to taste.
  4. Enjoy!
